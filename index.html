Your 8-Day Adventure

WELCOME TO ITALY!!!
01
Day 1 - Rome

Welcome to the Capital

Welcome to Italy! We’re starting in the Capital - Rome. Drop your bags, meet your crew, and get ready for the start of an epic 8-day journey through the heart of the Mediterranean.

LET'S EXPLORE ROME...
02
Day 2 - Rome

Walking Through History

Today we are going to get our bearings in Rome with a guided walking tour of the city's icons. Highlights include Colosseum Entry and a deep-dive tour of the ancient world's most famous arena.

FOODIE HEAVEN
03
Day 3 - Naples

Taste of the South

Are you ready for gooood food? Well, that’s great because we are headed to the food capital of the south. We'll embark on a Naples Street Food Tour to find the authentic flavors of the city.

DISCOVER POMPEII
04
Day 4 - Pompeii

Frozen in Time

One of the must-visit spots in Italy is Pompeii. We’ll spend the day exploring the hauntingly preserved ruins of this Roman city, learning about the lives cut short by Vesuvius.

HERCULANEUM & VIEWS
05
Day 5 - Sorrento

Wines & Orientation

This morning we are off to Herculaneum for a guided visit. Followed by an indulgent wine tasting session before we head to Sorrento for an orientation walk around our new coastal home.

PATH OF THE GODS
06
Day 6 - Amalfi Coast

Stairway to Heaven

The early bird catches the worm so we’re headed out early today to do the Path of the Gods Hike. We'll end the day visiting the picture-perfect Positano for some well-deserved relaxation.

CAPRI & APEROLS
07
Day 7 - Capri

Island Paradise

It’s our final full day on tour! We're visiting the glitzy island of Capri. We'll take the chairlift for incredible views and stroll through the Giardini di Augusto before a final group toast.

Day 8: Chilled Check Out!

It’s time to say Ciao... we’ll have one last breakfast together before heading our separate ways. Pasta la vista baby! You're a She Runs sister for life now. 💙
